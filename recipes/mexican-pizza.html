Mexican Pizza

Description

Mexican pizza is a Tex-Mex dish made with a flour tortilla topped with beans, meat, cheese, and other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef until evenly brown; drain.
  3. Mix in taco seasoning mix and refried beans.
  4. Spread beef mixture onto the pre-baked pizza crust.
  5. Top with Cheddar cheese, salsa, bell pepper, tomato, and green onions.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
