Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, coriander, chili powder, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, black pepper, and salt.
  2. Stir in chicken pieces until well coated.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add onion, garlic, and ginger; cook and stir until onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in marinated chicken and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in tomato sauce and cream.
  8. Simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened, about 30 minutes.
